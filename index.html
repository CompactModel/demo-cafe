Our Story

Coffee as a
daily ritual.

Brew & Soul opened in 2017 with one idea: that a great cup of coffee should be the best part of your morning. We source directly from farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia and Guatemala.

Every roast is done in-house, every recipe tasted daily. We believe in slow mornings and the kind of coffee that makes you stay a while.
